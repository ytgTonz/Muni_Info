About Muni-Info

Connecting communities with municipal services through technology

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between citizens and municipalities by providing a simple, accessible platform for reporting issues, tracking progress, and staying informed about local services.

Our Vision

To create empowered communities where every citizen has a voice and every municipal service issue is addressed efficiently and transparently through innovative technology solutions.

Key Features

Multi-Platform Access

Web portal, WhatsApp, USSD for feature phones - choose your preferred method

AI-Powered

Smart categorization and priority detection for faster, more accurate routing

Real-time Tracking

Track your complaint status and receive updates throughout the resolution process

Multi-language

Available in English, Afrikaans, isiZulu, and isiXhosa for broader accessibility

How It Works

1
Submit Your Complaint

Use our web portal, WhatsApp, or USSD to report any municipal service issue. Our AI system automatically categorizes and prioritizes your complaint.

2
Get Reference Number

Receive a unique reference number to track your complaint. This allows you to monitor progress and receive updates.

3
Smart Routing

Your complaint is intelligently routed to the appropriate department and assigned to the most suitable staff member.

4
Resolution & Feedback

Track progress, receive updates, and rate the service once resolved. Your feedback helps improve future service delivery.
